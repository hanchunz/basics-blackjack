Rules:
1. Get as close to 21 points as possible. The closest score wins!
2. You lose as soon as you go above 21 points.
3. Getting a blackjack wins you the game immediately! Blackjack involves getting a card worth 10 points (a ten, a Jack, a Queen, or a King) and an ace.
4. Aces can be worth either 1 or 11 points.
5. The dealer has to hit if their hand is below 17 points.
6. The dealer's first card is always dealt face down and concealed.

How to play:
1. The deck is shuffled and 2 cards are deal to each player, including the dealer.
2. Everyone's cards are dealt face up except for the dealer's first card, which is dealt face down and concealed.
3. If someone gets a blackjack here, they win and the game ends.
4. If there is no blackjack, the player now gets to decide if they want to hit (add a card into their hand) or stand (no further additions to their hand).
5. Once the player stands, the dealer hits if his total points is below 17, and stands if or when his total points is 17 or more.
6. The total points of each player are compared and the closest score to 21 but not over 21 wins!
